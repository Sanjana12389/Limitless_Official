Women In Education


What is the Problem?

For many years now, woman have fought for their rights. Some include their right to vote, to work, to have an education and much more. While it may seem that education is provided to those all around the world, it is still very hard for a girl to recive proper education. Countries such as Somalia, South Sudan and many more have very little access to school. In these countries, many girls arent granted primary school. This means, they start school at the age of 10 with very little resourses and material to study. This problem may seem little for now but it is still a growing issue and needs to be resolved.

How bad has this gotten?

To summarize this diagram, you could say that twice as many girls as boys will never start school. This is for a number of reasons. Some of which include:

  • Country doesnt offer school to girls
  • Parents feel that girls dont need education

    Who is Malala Yousafzai?

    Malala is a pakistani activist for woman's education. As a teenager, she demaded that girls have access to a proper education. Since then she has helped numerous schools and spread awareness on this issue. Malala has spoken at many events, written books, and acted in several movies showing the importance of female education.
