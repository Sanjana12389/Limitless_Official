How to Get Financial Aid

When it comes to getting financial help, we need places we can look to for help. Before you will be five Startup Grants Programs that will help you with a business idea.

The Small Business Innovation Research (SBIR) program offers the best federal small business grant for tech startups. Innovative tech startups are eligible for this grant which provides startups with funding to conduct research and development to aid a number of government institutions.

The US Economic Development Administration (EDA) offers the best state small business grant for startups. The EDA serves as a resource for startups of all types and sizes to find resources, grants, and other funding opportunities on a state level.

TheFedEx Small Business Grant Contest offers the best corporate small business grant for startup growth. FedEx offers one of the best-known small business grants with cash prizes and FedEx services awarded to the winners.

The Amber Grant is the best small business grant for women-owned startups. The Amber Grant is awarded monthly to women-owned startups in the US and Canada with an additional grant awarded to one of the 12 winners annually.

The Minority Business Development Agency (MBDA) offers the best small business grant for minority-owned startups. The MBDA provides an exhaustive list of resources for minority-owned startups to find and secure grants as well as other funding opportunities.
